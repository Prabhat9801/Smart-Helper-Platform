📱 How to Install on Your Device

🤖 Android (Chrome)

1
Open this page in Chrome browser
2
Tap the ⋮ menu (3 dots) in top right
3
Select "Add to Home screen"
4
Tap "Add" - App will appear on home screen!

🍎 iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1
Open this page in Safari browser
2
Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap "Add" - App icon will be on home screen!

💻 Windows/Mac (Chrome/Edge)

1
Open this page in Chrome or Edge browser
2
Look for Install icon (⊕) in address bar OR
3
Click ⋮ menu → "Install Smart Helper..."
4
Click "Install" - App will be in Start Menu/Applications!
💡 Pro Tip: Once installed, the app works offline and loads faster!
